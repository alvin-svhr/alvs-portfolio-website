PUT YOUR PHOTO HERE
===================

1. Drop your photo into this "assets" folder.
   A portrait-style image (taller than wide) looks best, e.g. 800 x 1000 px.

2. Open ..\data.js and set:
       profilePhoto: "assets/your-file-name.jpg",

That's it. If no photo is set, the site shows a gradient with your initials,
so it always looks clean.

You can also store project images and achievement photos in this folder and
reference them the same way, e.g. "assets/project-one.jpg".
